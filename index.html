Frequently asked questions.

What is a CDE and why do I need one?
A Common Data Environment is the single, controlled source of truth for project information — drawings, models, correspondence, approvals. It replaces email chains and shared drives with structured workflows, audit trails, and enforced review gates. For any project of meaningful size, a CDE isn't optional; it's the only way coordination holds together.
Is JKR BINAXONE compliant with ISO 19650 and JKR BIM standards?
Yes. The platform is built around ISO 19650 information states and JKR BIM standard deliverables, including BPEP structure, naming conventions, and transmittal workflows. Reviews, approvals, and digital signatures are captured in a form that maps directly to JKR audit expectations.
Where is my data stored?
On TM One Cloud Alpha, hosted in Cyberjaya, Malaysia — a Tier III data centre operated by a Malaysian vendor. Your project data is stored, processed, and backed up entirely within Malaysian jurisdiction.
What file formats are supported?
JKR BINAXONE is IFC-native and supports common open BIM formats, alongside standard document formats (PDF, DWG, DOCX, XLSX, images). Open formats are first-class citizens — not a second-tier export path.
How does JKR BINAXONE compare to other CDE platforms?
JKR BINAXONE is purpose-built around JKR workflows, Malaysian procurement practice, and local data-sovereignty requirements. You get strong technical capability alongside local hosting, support, and accountability — with licensing structures that align naturally with government and industry procurement in Malaysia.
Can JKR BINAXONE be deployed on-premise?
Yes. Enterprise engagements support dedicated tenancy on TM One Cloud Alpha, hybrid deployments, or full on-premise installation inside an agency's own data centre for the most sensitive programmes.
What does "post-quantum cryptography" mean for my documents?
Today's digital signatures can become forgeable once quantum computing matures. Infrastructure projects outlive the cryptography that signed them. Post-quantum algorithms are resistant to attacks from future quantum machines, which means a document signed in JKR BINAXONE today stays legally and technically verifiable decades from now — aligned with Malaysian national cybersecurity direction.
How is JKR BINAXONE priced?
Three tiers — Starter, Professional, Enterprise — each quoted to scope. Licensing can be structured as annual subscription or pay-per-project to match government procurement processes and private-sector contracts. Contact sales for a tailored proposal.
